=== NOTE on Jetpack's roles ===

Jetpack does not ship a custom role of its own (like WooCommerce's shop_manager).
The three provisioned roles (admin/compat_editor/compat_shop_manager) suffice.
In the disconnected/offline state (harness default), Jetpack grants capabilities
only to administrator. WordPress.com connection is required to grant Jetpack caps
to non-admin roles — not attempted per survey scope (DISCONNECTED only).
